─── Important after you get the external URL ────────────────────────────────
Better Auth requires BETTER_AUTH_BASE_URL to match the URL your browser uses.
Update it to the Minikube URL and redeploy:

  helm upgrade frontend ./frontend-chart \
    --set env.BETTER_AUTH_BASE_URL="http://<minikube-ip>:30000" \
    --set secrets.BETTER_AUTH_SECRET="<real-secret>" \
    --set secrets.DATABASE_URL="<real-db-url>"
